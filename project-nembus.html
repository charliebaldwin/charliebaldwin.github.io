NEMBUS
VIEW ON ARTSTATION

The third and final character for Lift-Off Laetus, Nembus. For this character, I wanted to design a heavy-hitting juggernaut, which I communicated through the broader silhouette and bulky armor pieces. I think that with each character I made for this project, I got progressively better with the workflow I was using, so in my opinion this design is the strongest, and I think the modeling came out a lot better as well.

Like the other characters from this project, the main sculpting was done in ZBrush, with supplementary modeling + retopology done in Maya, textured in Substance Painter, and rendered with UE4 and Marmoset.

If you want to try out Lift-Off Laetus, or just want to see more of our artistic process, head over to our game's site!
