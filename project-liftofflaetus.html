Game Summary
In this turn-based strategy game, take command of stranded astronaut crews as they clash over spare engine parts on a strange planet. Make use of resources from the environment around you to boost your own chances of survival or make youropponents’ much worse!
Supporting up to 6 players, two teams of 3 take turns navigating the grid-based map, moving engine cores back to their bases, and fending off the enemy using their arsenal of weapons. The first team to secure 3 cores wins!
Characters
  • Designed 3 different astronaut characters - Nembus, Pavo, and Lyra
  • Used distinct silhouettes for each to differentiate them during gameplay
  • Modeled in ZBrush, retopo & UVs in Maya
  • Rigged and animated in Mixamo, with skinning cleanup done in Maya
  • Modeled and textured space rifle used by characters
  • Textured in Substance Painter, created red/blue and green/yellow color schemes to differentiate teams
