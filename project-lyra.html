LYRA

The second character for Lift-Off Laetus, Lyra. To differentiate her from Pavo, I gave her more of a tall, slender silhouette. I also wanted have the design communicate what kind of soldier each character would be in the game world, so while Pavo boasts more of a standard commando look, for Lyra I gave her a backpack, helmet, and other gear becoming of some kind of field communications specialist, with the proper radio equipment needed to coordinate tactics on the battlefield.

Like Pavo, Lyra was sculpted in ZBrush, with supplementary elements and retopology done in Maya, textured in Substance Painter, and rendered with UE4 and Marmoset. Just as with Pavo, I decided to combine Emissive and Roughness into one texture. This new texture used RGB for the Emissive and Alpha for the Roughness, which is definitely unconventional but made the process of setting up materials in-engine a lot simpler for our tech team. You can see this texture lower down on the page.

If you want to try out Lift-Off Laetus, or just want to see more of our artistic process, head over to our game's site!
