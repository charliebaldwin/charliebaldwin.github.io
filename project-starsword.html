STAR SWORD

A star sword I made for a #swordtember Twitter challenge. This was a big exercise in getting down my Maya to ZBrush workflow, figuring out the best way to manage topology, high poly details, and baking textures. I also finally implemented Substance Designer into my workflow - I was trying to do the starry gem texture in my Painter project when I realized doing this with nodes would be way easier. I also ended up rendering this with IRay in Painter because for whatever reason the bloom effect I was getting there looked way better than when I put it into Marmoset.
