ABOUT

Microcosmonaut puts you in the shoes of a microbe, trying to navigate the dangers of the microcosmos in order to survive, reproduce, and evolve. The game rejects the typical 2D view of the microscopic world we're used to, perpetuated by our limited view into it through microscope slides, and envisions it as a vast and mysterious 3D space teeming with life.

  • Solo

Moonsighted is a PC third-person hack-and-slash adventure about cultural and political strife in the face of technology and progress amongst moon-worshipping moths trapped in a cavern. Play as Mairu, a moth miner using the power of cave crystals to empower himself and lift his people out of the depths of the earth.
