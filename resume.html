About ME
  Hey! I’m Charlie, a 3D artist passionate about games and the work that goes into making them beautiful. Currently looking to get a foothold in the industry with my first full-time or freelance position!
     I first dipped my toe into gaming at age 5, during the prime of Club Penguin. That was when I chose the username you'll still find on my ArtStation and Twitter, charlieatron. First-grade me wasn't the most creative, but it's managed to stick all these years. It wasn't until about age 12, when I bought my first proper game, Skyrim, that I realized that video games were something I was exceedingly passionate about. From that point on, I continued to play games to destress and to hang out with friends, trying and loving virtually every game genre under the sun. My gaming interests ranged from blockbuster FPS titles to emulated GBA Pokémon classics to complex 4X simulations and everything in between, and I was hooked.
‍
     When I got to high school, I was encouraged by a senior student to join his animation club, and my passing interest in 3D became a full-blown obsession. In the club we used Maya to make 6-minute short films, so at first I wanted to go into animation. But once I realized how the tools and techniques I was learning could be applied to building games, I knew exactly what I wanted to do with my life. Since then, I've been constantly expanding my repertoire of toolsets and learning how the games I love to play are built!
